Alignment

JFV Bookkeeping

  • https://jfvbookkeeping.com//

Alignment is an important part of a clear, organized and readable document. In particular, it helps the reader to look at a page and immediately know its purpose. Alignment can be used to organize information horizontally (left or right) or vertically (top down or bottom up).

Proximity

Critchfield

  • https://www.ccj.com/

The proximity principle is a common design technique that is used to create unity in visual designs. It's got two main characteristics: close proximity, as well as contrast or variation of elements within the composition. On this site, the related elements are organized into groups based on color.

Repetition

Momentum Energy

  • https://www.momentumenergy.com.au/

I can clearly see the principle of repetition, as an element is being repeated several times, which creates a feeling of organized movement and consistency within a composition. In turn, it creates an intrusion to promote the effectiveness of the product, which I believe is in other words, a marketing strategy, which gives more prestige and recognition to the site.
